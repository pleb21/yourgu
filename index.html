What's Your Poop Telling You?

This is not medical advice. Just poop-flavored trivia.

Type 1: Pebble Poop

Hard, separate lumps. Tough to pass. Probably constipated. Eat a vegetable maybe?

Type 2: Sausage Lumpy

Still pretty firm. A warning sign from your colon that it's working too hard.

Type 3: Cracked Sausage

This is... acceptable. Not great, not tragic. Like a C+ on a poop exam.

Type 4: Smooth Sausage

This is the gold standard. Your colon is living its best life.

Type 5: Soft Blobs

On the edge of diarrhea. Maybe too much coffee or nerves or both.

Type 6: Mushy Mess

Classic diarrhea. Could be food, stress, or your gut screaming for help.

Type 7: Liquid Lava

Full-blown diarrhea. Possibly demons. If it doesn’t stop, call someone qualified.
