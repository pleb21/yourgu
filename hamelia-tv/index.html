Setting temperature

Press TIMER button. Set hours using ▲▼, then press SET. AC will switch off automatically.

Troubleshooting

Not cooling?

Check mode is COOL, not fan. Temp below room temp.

Remote not working?

Spare batteries in the bedside drawer.

Unusual noise?

Please call host — do not continue running it.

water_heater

Water Heater (Geyser)

Step 1 — Switch on

Geyser switch is on the bathroom wall (red switch near the mirror). Turn it ON 10–15 minutes before your shower.

Step 2 — Wait

Indicator light turns off when water is hot enough. Usually 10 minutes for a good shower.

Step 3 — Switch OFF after use

Important: Always switch the geyser off after your shower. Do not leave it on overnight.

Good to Know

info

Capacity: approx. 15L. Enough for 1 long shower or 2 quick ones.

warning

Water pressure may be lower in the morning peak hours (7–9 AM).

Day Trips from Haldwani

terrain ~35 km

Nainital

The lake city. Naini Lake, Mall Road, boat rides. A classic — go in the morning to beat crowds.

forest ~75 km

Jim Corbett

India's oldest national park. Tiger safaris, bird watching, nature. Book ahead — popular year-round.

temple_hindu ~50 km

Mukteshwar

Hill station with Himalayan views, apple orchards, and a Shiva temple. Peaceful, less touristy.

landscape ~90 km

Kausani

Panoramic Himalayan views including Trisul & Nanda Devi. Gandhi called it the Switzerland of India.

water ~25 km

Bhimtal

Quieter lake town near Nainital. The lake has a small island with an aquarium. Very relaxed vibe.
